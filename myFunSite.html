The Chainsmokers

The Chainsmokers is an American DJ and production duo consisting of Alex Pall and Andrew Taggart.The Chainsmokers were re-formed as an EDM DJ duo in 2012 under the management of Adam Alpert in New York City. Pall, who had grown up DJing, was introduced to Taggart by Alpert, and had been working at an art gallery in Chelsea, Manhattan at the time.

Snails

Frédérik Durand (born 4 May 1988), professionally known as SNAILS, is a Canadian DJ and music producer. Durand's musical style has been described as "vomitstep", featuring "guttural bass gurgles, crushing 808s and synthesizers oscillated well past the point of absurdity" according to Billboard.

G.E.M

Jane Zhang
